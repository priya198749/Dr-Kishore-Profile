About Me

Dr. Kishore is a corporate finance professional and an economist with 25 years’ experience. He holds ranks in Ph.D (finance), MBA, M.com, and LLM; and is a qualified Company Secretary (ACS). Is an expert in corporate finance, project finance, PPP projects, venture capital, credit, legal, accounting, & HR.

His employments, and associations include Ocean Sparkle, Meghraj Bank, ICICI, SREI, IL&FS, Malaxmi, PRECA, Meenakshi, and BEKEM. Presently, he is serving as CFO & Director at BEKEM Infra Projects Pvt. Ltd. Previously, he served as Director and Committee Member of several organizations. He received Best CFO awards including from Economic Times ET Now Awards. He has professional memberships & associations with forums like CII, FTCCI, PRMIA - US, ICA, NHRD. He is Member - Board of Management of Pochampally Bank, one of the India’s top UCBs. He is part of ‘Africa Asia Forum on Corruption’, an International Organization headquartered in Indonesia with regional offices in 10 Asian & African countries working with Central Banks of various countries.

His other associations: Honorary Fellow of Dr.MCHRDI Govt of Telangana; Board Member at Trade Tower; Board of Studies Member at Woxsen International University; Board of Studies Member of Andhra Mahila Sabha Trust, Board Advisor for Startups, Financial Mentor @ Startup Grind - world’s largest Startup Community Platform (under Google & AWS); Vice President of Association of Valuation Professionals (AVP), a National Level organization head quartered at Mumbai; Member Board of Studies & Professor of Practice at SSIM an AICTE approved Business School, etc. He is a Member of World Association of PPP units and PPP professionals (WAPPP), a Geneva Switzerland based global organization promoting PPP projects. He is an Editorial Member of Banking Research Journals. He is a Jury Member for MMA Global based at London with 50 countries reach. He has also been Board Member of several other juries.

He delivered expert talks and case studies in various locations in India, Singapore, Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Malaysia, Vietnam, Indonesia, Thailand, and South Korea in person, and online in several countries. Delivered sessions for Civil Servants (IAS, IPS, IFS, ISS, IES), Commissioners, Govt Officials, Regulators, Bankers, Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Cost Accountants, Advocates, Investment Bankers, Stockbrokers, Management Professionals, Startups, Faculty; & at institutes including ISB, IIMs, Woxsen, NITIE, FIEO, NICMAR, Osmania University, FTCCI, GITAM, REVA, KLU, etc. He is frequently interviewed on TV channels, and other media on ‘financial aspects.’

Authored 85 + articles including in leading publications; guided 1000+ research projects. He has been championing Financial Literacy probono through FLAI. He is a humble Angel Investor. He is mentoring Startups, MSMEs, Scholars, and Professionals. He participated in many conferences.
